Events

The Cornell Table Tennis Club has events all year round, ranging from tryouts to a variety of tournaments. Every year, the Table Tennis Club participates in the NCTTA Upstate NY Divisional Tournament, where many teams from Upstate NY come to compete. Check out our upcoming and past events!

Upcoming Events

Event Date Description
YMCA Table Tennis Tournament April 29, 2019 Tournament including open singles and doubles divisions. Proceeds from the tournamnet will benefit the YMCA of Ithaca and Tompkins County Open Doors scholarship program.
NA Teams Table Tennis Tournament November 24, 2018 The largest table tennis competition in North America. The competition lasts for three days and allows individuals to play in teams.
NCTTA Upstate NY Divisional Tournament November 18, 2018 Annual competition where different collegiate teams from upstate New York come to compete.
