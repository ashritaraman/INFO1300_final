About

Who We Are

The Cornell Table Tennis Club is a student-run organization that competes at the divisional, regional, and national levels. We train several times per week, and our co-ed and women's teams are all nationally ranked. Anyone with an interest in playing table tennis are welcome to join the club and attend practices, regardless of skill level and experience.

Most importantly, we provide a space and community for members to enjoy and improve in Table Tennis. We aim to find committed players of all skill levels and are not limited to just the Cornell community.

For those in teams, our goal is to train, improve, and win NCTTA tournaments up to the National level. Tryouts occur every semester.
